• The Tale of the Deathly Hallows

    A Harry Potter Story

  • The meeting

    Three brothers encountered Death, who told them that he would reward them with whatever they wanted if they could defeat him.

  • The First Brother

    The first brother asked for a wand that could overpower any other wand, making him the most powerful wizard in the world. However, he became greedy and was killed by a man he taunted.

  • The Second Brother

    The second brother asked for a stone that could return his girlfriend from the dead. But when she returned, she was miserable and he killed himself out of grief.

  • The Third Brother

    The third brother, a humble man, asked for a cloak to hide him from death. He avoided death for many years under the cloak, then gave it to his son to meet Death as a friend.

  • Together

    So, the three gifts that were given to the brothers: the elder wand, the resurrection stone, and the cloak of invisibility make up the Deathly Hallows. With all of these things, one becomes the master of death.
